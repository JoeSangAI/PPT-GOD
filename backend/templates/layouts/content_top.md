## Layout: Content — Top Visual / 上图下文内容页

**Design Intent:** A strong visual statement at the top flows into structured text below, creating vertical rhythm and narrative progression.
<!-- 设计意图：顶部强有力的视觉陈述向下流入结构化文字，创造垂直节奏和叙事推进感。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- The upper visual must directly translate the content concept — cinematic, editorial, or architectural quality
- All text must be rendered clearly in the lower portion with excellent contrast
- Clear breathing space between visual and text areas
