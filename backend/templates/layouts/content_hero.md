## Layout: Content — Hero / 沉浸内容页

**Design Intent:** A cinematic, immersive moment where the visual dominates and text overlays with elegance and authority.
<!-- 设计意图：电影感、沉浸感的时刻，视觉主导，文字以优雅和权威感叠加其上。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- The background image IS the message — it must directly embody the content theme through dramatic lighting, spatial depth, or architectural presence
- All text must remain highly legible against the background (use overlays, contrast, or negative space as needed)
- Text can be minimal; let the visual breathe
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；背景图就是信息本身；所有文字必须高可读；文字可以精简，让视觉呼吸 -->
