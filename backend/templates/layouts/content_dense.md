## Layout: Content — Dense / 高密度内容页

**Design Intent:** Information is the hero. The visual recedes into a subtle atmospheric layer that supports reading without competing.
<!-- 设计意图：信息是主角。视觉退居为微妙的氛围层，支持阅读而不争抢。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- All text (headline, subhead, every line of body) must be rendered completely with no omissions — information density is high, so clarity is paramount
- Background should be subtle: texture, gradient, or faint thematic pattern only
- Generous spacing around text blocks; do not crowd
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；所有文字必须完整渲染无遗漏；背景必须微妙；文字块周围要有宽裕间距 -->
