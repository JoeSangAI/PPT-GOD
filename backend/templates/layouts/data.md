## Layout: Data / 数据页

**Design Intent:** Data-forward only when real data exists. The supplied numbers, table, or chart are the visual centerpiece, supported by clean typography.
<!-- 设计意图：只有存在真实数据时才数据优先。用户提供的数字、表格或图表是视觉中心，由干净排版支撑。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Use only numbers, labels, and relationships supplied by the slide content or references
- Chart visualization, table, or big number must be prominent and central
- Supporting labels and source notes are subtle, low contrast
- Data element needs breathing room; do not squeeze against edges
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；只使用页面内容或参考图提供的数据；图表、表格或大数字必须醒目居中；辅助标签低调；数据元素需要呼吸空间 -->
