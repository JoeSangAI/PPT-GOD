## Layout: Data / 数据页

**Design Intent:** Data-forward. The numbers or chart are the visual centerpiece, supported by clean typography.
<!-- 设计意图：数据优先。数字或图表是视觉中心，由干净的排版支撑。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Chart visualization or big number must be prominent and central
- Supporting labels and source notes are subtle, low contrast
- Data element needs breathing room; do not squeeze against edges
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；图表或大数字必须醒目居中；辅助标签低调；数据元素需要呼吸空间 -->
