## Layout: Hero / 金句页

**Design Intent:** A punchline slide: one memorable sentence, phrase, or word becomes the whole emotional beat. It can be a quote, slogan, conclusion, transition, data insight, or user voice, but it must still feel like the same deck.
<!-- 设计意图：金句页：一句话、一个短语或一个词成为整页情绪节拍。它可以是引用、口号、结论、转场判断、数据洞察或用户原话，但必须仍像同一套 PPT。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Keep typography from the same style family; do not introduce an unrelated display font
- One short sentence, phrase, or word dominates the frame; optional context/source is secondary
- Do not add body paragraphs, bullet lists, charts, or explanatory panels
- Background can be more stylized than a content slide: immersive photo, symbolic object, portrait, scene, or subtle texture/light
- Massive negative space. The text floats with authority
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；不突然换字体；一句短语主导画面，必要上下文/来源次级；不加正文、列表、图表；背景可更风格化；大量负空间，文字悬浮于权威感中 -->
