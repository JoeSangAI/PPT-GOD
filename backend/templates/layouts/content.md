## Layout: Content / 内容页

**Design Intent:** A premium content page within a business presentation. Design freely — treat it like a magazine editorial spread. There is no prescribed formula.
<!-- 设计意图：高端商务演示的内容页。自由设计，像杂志编辑排版一样。没有固定公式。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- All text (headline, subhead, every line of body) must be rendered clearly and legibly
- The background visual should directly translate the content concept into imagery — never generic decoration
- Generous whitespace and premium typographic hierarchy are essential
