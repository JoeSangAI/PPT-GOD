## Layout: Ending / 封底

**Design Intent:** A resolved closing slide. Echo the cover's tone, but quieter; close the conversation instead of opening a new argument.
<!-- 设计意图：收束明确的封底页。呼应封面的基调，但更安静；结束对话，而不是开启新论证。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Background visual echoes the cover's language but simplified
- Closing statement or call-to-action is centered and confident
- Do not introduce new evidence, charts, or dense explanatory content
- Sense of closure and finality
