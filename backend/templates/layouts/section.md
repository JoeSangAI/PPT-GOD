## Layout: Section Divider / 章节页

**Design Intent:** A chapter divider that gives the deck a clear pause and resets attention before the next argument.
<!-- 设计意图：章节分隔页，为下一段论证制造清晰停顿和节奏重置。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, typography, and material texture
- One section title, number, or short transition sentence dominates the frame
- Body copy should be empty or extremely short
- Use stronger color, negative space, texture, symbol, or scene mood than dense content pages
- Do not add bullet lists, charts, tables, or detailed proof points
