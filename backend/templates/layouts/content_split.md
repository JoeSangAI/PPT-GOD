## Layout: Content — Split / 分栏内容页

**Design Intent:** Text and visual share the canvas as equal partners. No rigid column split required — design freely like an editorial spread.
<!-- 设计意图：文字和视觉共享画布作为平等伙伴。不需要硬性的栏位分割——像编辑排版一样自由设计。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- All text (headline, subhead, every line of body) must be rendered clearly and legibly
- The visual should directly embody the content concept, not serve as generic decoration
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；所有文字清晰可读；视觉必须直接体现内容概念 -->
