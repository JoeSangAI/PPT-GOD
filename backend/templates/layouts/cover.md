## Layout: Cover / 封面

**Design Intent:** A commanding first impression. Full-bleed visual with overlaid title text. The image sets the emotional tone for the entire deck.
<!-- 设计意图：令人印象深刻的第一印象。全幅视觉叠加标题文字。图像为整份演示定下情感基调。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Background visual fills the canvas — cinematic, architectural, or editorial quality
- Title and subtitle must be highly legible against the background (dark overlay, gradient, or negative space as needed)
- No isolated floating elements; everything feels grounded and intentional
