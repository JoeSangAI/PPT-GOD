## Layout: Table of Contents / 目录

**Design Intent:** An optional navigation slide for strongly structured decks. It should feel like a calm roadmap, not a decorative menu.
<!-- 设计意图：只服务强结构内容的可选导航页。它应该像清爽路线图，而不是装饰菜单。 -->

**Constraints:**
- Maintain the style system's palette, mood, and material texture
- Use only when the deck needs navigation; omit it for narrative-first decks
- 3-6 short section titles and numbering are the primary focus
- Background is minimal: subtle gradient, texture, or thin dividing lines
- Generous spacing between items; do not crowd or add icon clutter
<!-- 约束：保持风格系统；叙事型 deck 可省略；3-6 个短章节名和编号是主要焦点；背景极简；条目间间距宽裕，不堆图标 -->
