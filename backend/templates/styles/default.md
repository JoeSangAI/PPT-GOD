## Style System (English for model / 中文供维护)

**Theme:** Modern business presentation, professional and clean.
<!-- 主题：现代商务演示，专业简洁 -->

**Mood:** Confident, trustworthy, calm authority.
<!-- 氛围：自信、可信赖、沉稳的权威感 -->

**Color Palette:**
- Primary deep navy `#1E3A5F` — headers, key elements
- Warm cream `#F5F5F0` — backgrounds, breathing space
- Accent warm tan `#D4A574` — highlights, emphasis
- Secondary blue `#2C5282` — supporting elements
- Light stone `#E8E4DF` — subtle dividers
<!-- 配色：深海军蓝主色，暖奶油背景，暖棕点缀，二级蓝辅助 -->

**Texture & Material:**
- Matte paper texture, subtle grain
- Soft shadows, no heavy 3D
- Clean edges, generous whitespace
<!-- 质感：哑光纸纹，柔和阴影，无厚重3D，边缘干净，大量留白 -->

**Art Direction:**
- Editorial photography style when images are needed
- Abstract geometric shapes as decorative elements
- Minimal line art for icons and illustrations
<!-- 艺术方向：编辑摄影风格，抽象几何装饰，极简线描图标 -->

**Font:** DM Sans (a clean, modern geometric sans-serif)
<!-- 字体：DM Sans，干净现代的几何无衬线体 -->

**Typography Feel (describe only, do not render text):**
- Strong serif or clean sans-serif for headlines
- Clear hierarchy through size and weight contrast
- Ample line spacing
<!-- 排版感：标题用强衬线或简洁无衬线，层级通过大小粗细区分，行距宽松 -->

**Composition Principles:**
- Rule of thirds or centered focal point
- Asymmetric balance preferred
- 15-25% margin breathing room on all sides
<!-- 构图原则：三分法或中心焦点，非对称平衡，四周留出15-25%边距 -->
